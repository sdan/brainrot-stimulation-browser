Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2024

Thank you for choosing to use Brainrot Stimulation for ChatGPT. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how this extension (“we,” “our,” or “us”) handles any data it processes.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the Brainrot Stimulation for ChatGPT browser extension (the “Extension”). It does not apply to any external websites, services, or platforms (e.g., YouTube) that this Extension may load or link to. Your use of third-party services is governed by their respective privacy policies and terms.

2. Data Collection and Use

  • No Personal Data Collection: The Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or personally identifiable data. It merely injects video iframes for display alongside the ChatGPT interface.
  • Permissions: We use the activeTab permission to inject overlay content into supported websites. This permission does not give us access to your browsing history or personal data. The Extension only activates when you visit the domains specified in the Extension’s manifest file.
  • External Content: The Extension loads video embeds from YouTube and other external hosts. Please note that using such external services is subject to their own privacy policies and terms of service; we do not control or receive any personal data from these external hosts.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Since we do not collect user data, we do not share, sell, or disclose your personal information with any third parties.

4. Security

We do not collect or store user data on our servers. Any interactions with external sites (e.g., YouTube) occur over secure connections as provided by those platforms. We recommend that you always maintain good security practices, including keeping your browser and operating system up to date.

5. Children’s Privacy

This Extension is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technologies, legal requirements, or other factors. Any updates will be posted here with an updated “Last updated” date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the Extension, please reach out to us at brainrot.dev@sdan.cc.

By installing or using the Brainrot Stimulation for ChatGPT Extension, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
